Cutting through chaos: how I organize Telegram

Dec 30, 2025

While I'm procrastinating ruminating on my end-of-year review, I thought it'd be fun to show off how I organize Telegram.

My chat organization scheme has developed naturally over time, but if I had to come up with principles that I use to organize them:

  • Prioritization. The bulk of my Telegram folders are manually curated, and the bulk of those have pinned chats.
  • Object permanence. I make use of spatial memory where relevant.
